The Ceremony

Wedding Ceremony

The ceremony will be held on the beach, in front of the 5th Street entrance, which is only a few blocks from the reception venue. There is a pavilion there overlooking the beach, and our guests are welcome to view the ceremony from there, or down on the sand with us. The dress code is "summer casual", so suit and tie are not required!

When
Saturday, September 17th, 2016 4:30pm
Where
5th Street beach access
Beach Haven, NJ 08008

Reception

After the ceremony, guests are invited to walk or drive back to The Island Guest House's garden area for drinks and hors d'oeuvres. The reception will be from 6:00pm to about 10:00pm, after which everyone is invited to join us down on the beach for a special chinese latern lighting event!

Saturday, September 17th, 2016 6:00pm - 10:00pm
Where
The Island Guest House
