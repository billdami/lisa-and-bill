Travel & Accommodations

The wedding reception is being held at The Island Guest House bed & breakfast, and the bridal party along with immediate family will be staying there Friday the 16th through Sunday the 18th.

Since the venue itself has limited space, we have reserved room blocks for the rest of our guests at two other nearby hotels, The Engleside Inn and Coral Seas Oceanfront Motel. Both hotels are within walking distance, only a few blocks from the venue. When booking a room, please mention that you are attending Lisa Schmidt and Bill Dami's wedding, to get one of our reserved rooms.
